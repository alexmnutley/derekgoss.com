about

Hi there! My name is Derek Goss. I’m a rising 9th grader who will be attending Harriton High School this fall.
I’m an aspiring developer and entrepreneur, and I love coding my own applications and websites – in fact, I made this whole website myself!

When I’m not working on a project, you can find me running cross-country, playing lacrosse, sailing, biking, wearing sunglasses, traveling the world, hanging out in a coffee shop, or cheering on the Tar Heels and the Cornell Big Red.

I’m thrilled that you’re here, so please feel free to look around! In the coming months I hope to be adding some of my own personal projects to the Project section of this website, including an upcoming Android application, and some scripts written in Python for everyday computing and word processing. You can find links to my Instagram and Github pages in the Media section, which will also contain articles and press when that content becomes available. If you have any questions or comments (and I mean any), please find my email in the Contact section and I will be more than happy to respond. Away from your computer? Fear not, for this website is also available in mobile versions for phones and tablets!

By now, I hope you’ve learned a little something about me, and I hope you enjoy the rest of your time here. As a reward for reading this far, I’ll leave you with this gem: what kind of clothes do lawyers wear? Lawsuits.
